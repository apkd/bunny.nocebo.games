GOT CARROTS?

Bunny Game is a challenging survival game focused on cooperation.

Get together with friends and work as a team to last as long as possible and collect resources to prepare for winter.

You have to use your wit, creativity, and logic to survive this dangerous world. It's an intense, fun experience that will keep you coming back for more!

ONLINE 4-PLAYER CO-OP

Play online with friends or on your own if you're feeling shy.

The more, the merrier! Work together to overcome obstacles and gather resources. The difficulty of the challenges you encounter is scaled to the number of players.

THINK AHEAD

You'll need to work together if you want to survive past winter.

Prepare by collecting resources and bringing them to your home burrow.

PREPARE CAREFULLY

Challenges lie ahead - move carefully and work together to survive.

Spend the resources you gathered to unlock tools and upgrades which will make your job easier. Unlock new maps and areas for you and your friends to explore.

DON'T GET CAUGHT

The locals don't take kindly to carrot-stealing visitors.

Sneak around using burrows and thick vegetation. Move swiftly under the cover of night.

WINTER IS COMING

Cold slows you down and makes you vulnerable.

Stay warm!

TIME AND WEATHER

Prepare and survive against dangerous weather changes!

Mind the time and don't get lost at night!
